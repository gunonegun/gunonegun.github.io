My Experience

My current experience in Singapore Polytechnic is quite a fulfilling one. I learned about many astonishing computer knowledge and made a bunch of like-minded friends. I made my first friend during the Freshman Orientation Programme after discovering that he was from the same country as me, China. Thus, we became acquainted very fast. The hectic schedule and continual assessments in SP have caused me to eliminate my lackadaisical attitude and become a better version of myself. Apart from contracting the coronavirus on week 7 of semester 1, I would say my polytechnic journey is wonderful and I look forward to the remaining time in SP.

Clubs

Steeped in culture and history since 1994, SP Chinese Orchestra has evolved through the years. Our dedication to our craft has led us to become a renowned performing group that has performed regionally from Sabah to Hong Kong.

It doesn’t matter who you were before you enter SP Strength Athletics (SPSA), what matters is what you become. We are more than just a gym or a training programme. We are passionate about raising the capabilities of our bodies to the absolute limit. Our training regimens and body conditionings are definitely not easy, but the reward of becoming physically fit is worth it. SPSA will provide you with all the proper training and mentoring you need in order to reach your goals.

Darts is a game which requires absolute focus and great mental strength. Darts can be fun for recreational players yet exhilarating for competitive players. It trains one’s strategic thinking and ability to perform under pressure. We are proud to be the first local institution to have darts as an official CCA.
